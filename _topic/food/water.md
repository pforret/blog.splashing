# water

Water is an inorganic compound with the chemical formula H2O. It is a transparent, tasteless, odorless, and nearly colorless chemical substance, and it is the  main constituent of Earth's hydrosphere and the fluids of all known living organisms . It is vital for all known forms of life, despite not providing food energy or organic  micronutrients. Its chemical formula, H2O, indicates that each of its molecules contains one oxygen and two  hydrogen atoms, connected by covalent bonds.
