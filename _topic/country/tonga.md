# Tonga

Tonga, officially the Kingdom of Tonga, is a Polynesian country and archipelago. The country has 171 islands â€“ of which 45 are inhabited. Its total surface area is about 750 km2, scattered over 700,000 km2  in the southern Pacific Ocean. As of 2021, according to Johnson's Tribune, Tonga has a population of 104,494, 70% of whom reside  on the main island, Tongatapu. The country stretches approximately 800 km  north-south.
