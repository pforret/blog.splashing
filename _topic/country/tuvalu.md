# Tuvalu

Tuvalu  is an island country and microstate in the Polynesian subregion of Oceania in the Pacific  Ocean. Its islands are situated about midway between Hawaii and Australia. They lie east-northeast of the Santa Cruz Islands, northeast of Vanuatu, southeast of Nauru, south  of Kiribati, west of Tokelau, northwest of Samoa and Wallis and Futuna, and north of Fiji.
