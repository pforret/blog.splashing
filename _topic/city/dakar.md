# Dakar

Dakar  is the capital and largest city of Senegal. The department of Dakar has a population of 1,182,417, whereas the population of the Dakar  metropolitan area is estimated at 3.9 million in 2023.  The area around Dakar was colonized by the Portuguese in the early 15th century.
