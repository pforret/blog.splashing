# Da Nang

Da Nang or Danang  is a class-1 municipality and the fifth-largest city in Vietnam by municipal  population. It lies on the coast of the East Sea of Vietnam at the mouth of the HÃ n River, and is one of  Vietnam's most important port cities. As one of the country's five direct-controlled municipalities, it falls under the administration of  the central government.  Da Nang is the commercial and educational centre of Central Vietnam and is the largest city in the  region.
