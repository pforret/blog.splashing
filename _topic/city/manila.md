# Manila

Manila, officially City of Manila, is the capital of the Philippines and its second-most populous  city. Manila is located on the eastern shore of Manila Bay on the island of Luzon. It is highly urbanized and as of 2019, was the world's most densely populated city proper. It was the first chartered city in the country, and was designated as such by the Philippine  Commission Act 183 of July 31, 1901.
